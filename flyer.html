Attractions in Singapore Flyer

Singapore Flight

Experience breathtaking, panoramic views on Asia’s largest observation wheel. For locals and tourists alike, there is only one place in Singapore that offers a bird’s eye view of the entire island city.

Singapore Sling Flight

Singapore Sling is a world-renowned cocktail drink. For a perfect memory of your visit to Singapore, enjoy a glass of original Singapore Sling onboard Singapore Flyer. Privileges include Express Boarding.

Singapore Flyer Sky Dining

Indulge in our Singapore Flyer Sky Dining, which comes with a 4-course menu, in-flight host, and magnificent skyline views in the comfort of a spacious capsule, atop Asia’s Largest Observation Wheel.

Premium Champagne Flight

The Premium Champagne Flight is the ideal choice to pamper those you care about or want to impress, whether it is family, friends or discerning business associates.
