Wildlife Reserves Singapore (WRS) is dedicated to the management of world-leading zoological institutions—Jurong Bird Park, Night Safari, River Safari and Singapore Zoo—that aim to inspire people to value and conserve biodiversity by providing meaningful and memorable wildlife experiences.

Three Little Pigs: A Lunar Adventure Story Walk

Discover the wildest version of the Three Little Pigs and the “Big Bad” Wolf at our Story Walk! Celebrate Chinese New Year with our adorable Mini Pigs, Babirusa and Warthog at Singapore Zoo.

Once Upon a River: Lunar New Year Special

Every river has many stories to tell. Join us for a festive edition of our show and discover fascinating creatures by the river. Look out for auspicious numbers picked by our Grey Parrot, Lowe!

The Pig Who Wanted to Fly: A Lunar Adventure Story Walk

LOnce upon a time, a mini pig really wished to fly. So he went on an adventure to make his dream come true. Find out what happens next in our fantastic self-guided story walk at Jurong Bird Park!

Festive Animal Enrichments

Catch our animals in action! See how they sharpen their instincts with festive treats specially prepared by our keepers.

Thumbuakar Performance: Lunar New Year Special
